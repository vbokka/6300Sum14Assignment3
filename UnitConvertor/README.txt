
Installing on phone:
Download and Install UnitConvertor.apk file from UnitConvertor\bin directory on to your Android phone.

From eclipse right click on UnitConvertor project and click on Runas android application

Usage:


App  has three buttons - 

Temperature -  To convert temperature fom  Farienheit to Celsius and vice versa. You can input negative temperatures
Distance    -  To convert distance from Kilometers to Miles and Miles to Kilometers.
Weight      -  To convert weight from pounds to Kilograms and kilograms to pounds.


Input  a number and click the radio button and the app will convert to the units that has been selected by the user.
